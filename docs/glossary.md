### Affinity
:	an soul's purity of essence of a certain Aspect. A portion is innately determined by one's spirit roots; however latent affinity can be realized through deeper understanding of a concept.

### Aspect
:	a conceptual characteristic or function of reality, representing a pattern of phenomena in the world.

### Astral Plane/Space
:	layer of reality in which spirits reside.

### Aura
:	emitted residue from mana reflecting off the spirit and soul.

### Blood Essence
:	physical representation of a creature's soul essence. Created by extracting an entity's vitality of their body in combination with their soul essence.

### Bloodline Essence
:	distilled, high-purity blood essence of a race's progenitor. Upon consumption and awakening, expands the recipient's spirit roots, rewriting their genetic code. Like other affinities, latent bloodline potential must be awakened through soulscape sculpting.

### Casting (Magic)
:	using one’s essence/soul to shape mana into an effect on the physical world, bending physical laws. Magical phenomena is manifested by essence as a “substance blueprint” and the caster’s will as a “form blueprint”.

### Channeling (Soul)
:	the practice of leveraging one's soulscape and affinities to cast magic.

### Divinity
:	the purity of one's soul essence; how refined one's soul essence is towards a self-concept.

### Dreamsculpting
:	Soulscape cultivation technique. Using lucid dreams (or advanced meditation) to access one's subconscious; with consistent and deliberate shaping, one can impact the shape of their soulscape.

### Ego
:	the sense of self/personality; a characteristic of sapience. Usually resides in the mind, developed by conscious experiences. With a powerful enough will, may be imprinted upon one's soul.

### Embodiment (Soul)
:	the practice of shaping one's soulscape to match and synchronise with one's body, allowing one to enhance and manipulate the body's state based on their soul.

### Entanglement (Soul)
:	the practice of using one's soulscape to connect with and/or manipulate another entity. Used to borrow power from higher beings or influence lower beings. Often involves creating a "gate" in one's spirit membrane and allowing connection to another entity.

### Essence
:	ethereal potentia embedded with Aspects. that which powers the soul and shapes phenomena. Null essence is the energy of reality without any embedded aspects; rare in nature and difficult to manage. Mixed essence is most common: impure mix of many essences, diluted in null essence. Essence purity is measured by its highest constituant concept. Complex essence is a mixed essence which has woven together certain essences to produce an artificial purity. Pure essence represents a single concept; usually woven and refined from a lower-purity complex essence.

### Ether/Aether
:	world in which souls and essence reside. A space accessible across all realities.

### Fate
:	ethereal kinetic energy; phenomena and patterns that influence souls and reality. (The "Winds of Fate" blow across the ether. The "Strings of Fate" tie souls together.)

### Mind
:	the most complex part of the spirit root which enables rational thinking, residing in the brain. Is formed by emergent behavior resulting from a soul interacting with the brain, and is thus damaged when either the soul or brain is damaged. The soul is the "energy" powering the mind, while the brain is the "hardware" of the mind. Slowly adapts/shapes the soul based on conscious perception of self and the world.

### Phylactery
:	inanimate object housing a soul.

### Reality
:	the material plane of existence. Provides grounding for worlds and entities in the ether.

### Realm
:	an ether-entity embedded with a system of laws, allowing it to house independent souls and subsist in the ether. These laws define how it creates internal changes (potential futures -> present) through ingestion of ethereal potential (essence) and movement (fate). Must be either housed/powered by a soul or anchored to one to remain stable.

### Sentience
:	the ability to experience subjective experiences (qualia). Created by the soul.

### Soul
:	cell of shaped essence that defines an individual. Requires a spirit membrane to resist dissolution in the ether.

### Soulseed
:	a rare capsule of highly compressed form of pure essence. Refined from a powerful soul over long time periods and specific circumstances; can act as an inheritance from a deceased powerful entity. May contain parts of the generating soul's ego, intuitive understanding of the essence's concept, and/or resonant spirit frequencies.

### Soulscape
:	the visualization of one's soul.

### Soul essence
:	the base essence within one's soul, one's "essence of self". The unique energy that identifies one within the world.

### Spell
:	a defined form/blueprint (written: runes, spoken: chants/incantation) by which magic can be cast.

### Spirit
:	the boundary between an entity's soul and reality. Is composed of the sprit membrane and spirit root.

### Spiritcell
:	a pseudo-soul attached to an object or entity, created by imbuing that object with essence and wrapping it with a spirit membrane.

### Spiritualism (Soul)
:	the practice of using one's soulscape to manipulate one's spirit in order to commune and resonate with other spiritual beings.

### Spirit membrane
:	a part of the spirit which acts as a boundary around the soul itself, to preserve it within the ether.

### Spirit root(s)
:	the part(s) of the spirit which anchors it to reality; the "bridge" between the body and soul.

### Spirit beast
:	an animal that developed a consciousness and spirit (typically over a long time period), and subsequently formed a soul.

### Spirit domain
:	a Mage/Channeler's spirit manifestation, an outer layer of spirit. Allows one to cast magic inside it.

### Will
:	the "engine" of the soul, developed in the soul by the mind, advanced through a sense of meaning and purpose; propels dynamicity and change in the soul. Encompasses base principles, desires, and in some cases, (parts of) the ego imprinted from the mind.

### Willpower
:	the capacity to draw upon one's soul essence; the aspect of the spirit root determining "flow rate" from the Will to the body.

### World
:	a realm whose system is self-sustaining through its anchoring to reality / the material plane. Through that anchoring, worlds are ever-growing, retaining history of the past.
